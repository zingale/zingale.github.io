Double Detonation Models of Type Ia Supernovae

We are modeling the double detonation scenario for SN Ia. Here, a He detonation begins in the accreted layer on a sub-Chandrasekhar mass white dwarf, driving a compression wave inward that ignites a second, C detonation near the center.
